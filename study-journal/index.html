What we do

"The Gospel Study Journal" is a digital platform that invites Latter-day Saints to share their insights, spiritual experiences, and testimonies of the Gospel. It serves as a public diary where individuals can contribute one thing they have learned, recount one spiritual experience, and express the purpose behind their sharing. This platform aims to foster collaboration, learning, and unity among the Latter-day Saint community. By engaging in open dialogue and sharing personal revelations, we can deepen our understanding, strengthen our collective testimony, and inspire one another on our spiritual journeys. Join us in this transformative endeavor as we bear witness to the light and truth of the Gospel of Jesus Christ.
